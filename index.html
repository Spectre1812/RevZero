The Fuel System — Flex-Fuel Strategy + Carbon-Catch

APEX supports flex-fuel operation with sensors and ECU maps that adapt to ethanol content. Carbon-Catch captures particulate carbon in a replaceable Apex-1 cartridge.

How flex-fuel works
  • Fuel composition sensor reports blend percentage to the ECU.
  • ECU applies calibrated maps for ignition, boost, and injector pulse width.
  • Ethanol-compatible materials and dedicated refuelling port ensure reliability.

Turbocharger, Direct Injection & VVT

Turbo forces dense air; direct injection meters fuel; VVT optimizes breathing across the rev range.

Higher ethanol content allows more aggressive ignition and boost maps for stronger midrange torque.

Fuel & Carbon-Catch Technology — APEX

APEX's fuel system is designed for flex-fuel operation. The Carbon-Catch unit captures particulate carbon in a replaceable Apex-1 cartridge.

Flex-Fuel Benefits & Handling

Flex-fuel capability lets owners choose fuels based on availability and priorities: maximum performance with high-ethanol blends, or longer range with petrol.

  • High ethanol content enables higher safe boost and more aggressive ignition timing.
  • Petrol offers higher energy density per liter, improving range.
  • ECU automatically adjusts fueling and timing based on ethanol sensor readings.

Carbon-Catch — Visible Emissions Management

The Carbon-Catch unit captures particulate carbon in a replaceable Apex-1 cartridge. The cartridge is user-serviceable and recyclable.

User flow
  1. Inspect the Carbon-Catch window on the rear diffuser.
  2. Open the side-loading Apex-1 cartridge bay; remove and replace the pellet cartridge.
  3. Return used cartridges to authorized recycling centers for processing.
